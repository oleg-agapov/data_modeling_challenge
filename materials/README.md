# Additional Materials

A curated list of books, videos, courses, and guides about data modeling.


## Books

- 📖 [**Agile Data Warehouse Design: Collaborative Dimensional Modeling, from Whiteboard to Star Schema**](https://www.amazon.ca/Agile-Data-Warehouse-Design-Collaborative/dp/0956817203)
    
  _Lawrence Corr & Jim Stagnitto, 2011 (~12–20 hours)_
  
  Workshop-oriented book that walks through translating business requirements into dimensional structures quickly and collaboratively.

- 📖 [**The Data Warehouse Toolkit: The Definitive Guide to Dimensional Modeling**](https://www.amazon.ca/Data-Warehouse-Toolkit-Definitive-Dimensional/dp/1118530802/ref=asc_df_1118530802)
  
  _Ralph Kimball & Margy Ross, 2013 (~25–45 hours)_
  
  The canonical dimensional modelling reference. Covers grain, facts, dimensions, SCD patterns, and role-playing dimensions with consistent vocabulary and real case studies.

- 📖 [**The Data Warehouse Lifecycle Toolkit**](https://www.amazon.ca/Data-Warehouse-Lifecycle-Toolkit/dp/0470149779/ref=sr_1_1)
  
  _Ralph Kimball, Margy Ross, Warren Thornthwaite, Joy Mundy & Bob Becker, 2008 (~25–45 hours)_
  
  Covers how dimensional modelling fits into iterative delivery, requirements capture, and stakeholder alignment. Best for intermediate learners.


## Videos and Courses

- ▶️ [**Back to the Future: Where Dimensional Modeling Enters the Modern Data Stack**](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-yQa_DxEqaQ)

  _dbt Coalesce 2022, 44 mins_
  
  A modern defence of dimensional modelling connecting classic star-schema thinking to today's tooling and team structures. Good for team alignment.
  


- 🧑‍🎓 [**Analytics Engineering with dbt Specialization**](https://www.coursera.org/specializations/analytics-engineering-with-dbt)
  
  _Coursera (3-course series) (~40 hours)_
  
  Structured programm covering facts, dimensions, and star schemas in the context of modern analytics engineering (dbt, testing, documentation, BI delivery).


## Online Guides and Documentation

- 🖥️ [**Kimball Dimensional Modeling Techniques**](https://www.kimballgroup.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/2013.09-Kimball-Dimensional-Modeling-Techniques11.pdf) (PDF)

  _Kimball Group, 2013 (~1–2 hours)_
  
  Compact official reference enumerating core dimensional modelling patterns and vocabulary (SCD variants, conformed dimensions, etc.). A great checklist and shared-language document.

- 🖥️ [**Understand star schema and the importance for Power BI**](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/power-bi/guidance/star-schema)
  
  _Microsoft Learn, 2024 (~30–60 min)_
  
  Clear explanation of star schema relevance for semantic models, including when snowflaking is acceptable. Good vendor-backed intro for BI-focused learners.

- 🖥️ [**Building a Kimball dimensional model with dbt**](https://docs.getdbt.com/blog/kimball-dimensional-model)
  
  _dbt Developer Blog, 2023 (~30–60 min)_
  
  Practical walkthrough connecting classic Kimball ideas to dbt project structure. Covers implementing dimensional models in SQL transformations.
  

- 🖥️ [**A complete guide to dimensional modeling with dbt**](https://www.getdbt.com/blog/guide-to-dimensional-modeling)
  
  _dbt Blog, 2025 (~30–60 min)_
  
  Beginner-friendly conceptual intro to dimensional modelling from a Kimball/analytics-engineering perspective.

- 🖥️ [**What Is a Star Schema? A Complete Guide for Data Modeling**](https://www.snowflake.com/en/fundamentals/star-schema/)
  
  _Snowflake, 2026 (~15–30 min)_
  
  Accessible primer on star schema components, trade-offs, and star vs snowflake schema comparison.
